About EduAll

Our Story and Mission

Who We Are

EduAll is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing quality education to children in rural Uganda. Founded in 2020, we've been working tirelessly to bridge the educational gap between urban and rural areas.

Our Mission

Our mission is to ensure that every child in Uganda, regardless of their location or economic background, has access to quality education. We believe that education is the key to breaking the cycle of poverty and creating sustainable development.

What We Do

We implement various programs to achieve our mission:

School Building Initiative

We construct and equip schools in underserved rural areas, providing a safe and conducive learning environment for children.

Teacher Training Program

We provide comprehensive training to local teachers, enhancing their skills and teaching methodologies.

Scholarship Program

We offer scholarships to talented but underprivileged students, covering their tuition, books, and other educational expenses.

Digital Learning Initiative

We introduce technology in classrooms, providing computers and internet access to bridge the digital divide.
